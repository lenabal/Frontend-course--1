Jedi Knights

Obi Wan Kenobi

Obi-Wan Kenobi, a wise and skilled Jedi Knight, is one of the most iconic characters in the Star Wars universe. Known for his calm demeanor, sharp wit, and strong sense of duty, Obi-Wan played a crucial role in the training of Anakin Skywalker and later his son, Luke Skywalker. Throughout his journey, Obi-Wan faced numerous challenges, from battling Sith Lords to navigating the complexities of war and betrayal. His unwavering commitment to the Jedi Order and his deep understanding of the Force made him a legendary figure, whose legacy continues to inspire hope in the galaxy.
